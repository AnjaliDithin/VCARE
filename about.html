What we do


V Care International repatriation Services in UAE based professional firm working under Dubai Economy Department project of Value Care Project Management, which provides the Funeral Services in UAE- the returning of a deceased person to their country of origin following an overseas death of any culture, religion or country of destination. Value Care International Repatriation Services specializing in funeral services and international repatriation. Becoming the fundamental support for insurance, assistance and Local Companies or authorities, funeral companies as well as consulates and families around the world and especially in UAE.

Our specialized experience in the integral management of funeral services, international repatriations, and our extensive national and international funeral network, we can offer a complete, high quality and personalized service that meet their needs irrespective of the location.
